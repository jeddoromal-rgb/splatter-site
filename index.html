Weekly Cycle Workflow

Splatter runs in weekly cycles. Each step is shown below with role ownership and approval gates.
Monday
Input Curation
Product Owner (Maria)
Gather approved case proof, delivery insights, positioning guardrails, and priority themes for the week. Update the input file that Splatter reads.
Monday
Visibility Plan
Splatter (AI)Approval Gate
Based on curated inputs, Splatter proposes topics, formats, target keywords, and backlink targets for the week. PO reviews plan before generation begins.
Tue–Wed
Draft Generation
Splatter (AI)
Splatter produces all drafts: keyword research, 1–2 blog articles, 3 LinkedIn posts, backlink target list, and outreach email drafts. All outputs saved locally.
Thursday
Product Owner Review
Product Owner (Maria)Approval Gate
Maria reviews all outputs using the Review Checklist. She can approve, request edits, or reject. Outputs that need founder input are flagged.
Thu–Fri
Founder Gate (if needed)
Founders (Jesse + Team)Approval Gate
Only triggered for strategic content, sensitive claims, or high-visibility outputs. Founders approve or redirect. This gate protects credibility without creating bottlenecks.
Friday
Manual Deployment
Product Owner (Maria)
Approved blog posts are published to CMS. LinkedIn posts are posted by founders or PO. Outreach emails are sent manually. Nothing is automated.
Friday
KPI Logging
Product Owner (Maria)
Record outputs produced, approval rates, and any qualitative notes. Update the weekly dashboard. Flag improvements for next cycle.
